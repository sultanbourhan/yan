YANA FILONENKO

MAYANAMODEL

Originally from Ukraine, I’ve had the pleasure of bringing my talents to the dynamic city of Barcelona, where I currently reside. My journey in the world of modeling and acting has been fueled by a deep love for the arts and a desire to tell stories through my work. In Spain, I've had the honor of starring in a leading role in a music video, working on a short film with a renowned Spanish director, and featuring in commercials for well-known Spanish brands. My multicultural background and fluency in both English and Spanish allow me to connect with a broad audience and adapt to various roles and styles. I am dedicated to my craft, constantly evolving, and always open to new collaborations and creative challenges. Whether it's a photoshoot, a film project, or an artistic experiment, I approach every opportunity with enthusiasm and a desire to exceed expectations.
